Internships

  • Earn while you learn
  • Gain experience, insight, and skills for the future
  • Tackle live projects, and make a valuable contribute to our work

Ideal for ambitious students with a readiness to learn and get involved, we're committed to providing high quality work experience to high achievers at various stages of academia. Our sought-after internship programmes offer an excellent opportunity to gain experience and contribute to work directly impacting the UK's economy.

First Year Internship

Our new First Year Internship scheme is designed for undergraduate students in their first year interested in finding more about the Bank. These internships run for six weeks giving you the opportunity to support a team on a specific project across all areas of the Bank, from procurement or HR to monetary policy and banking supervision. During this time, you’ll be paid a salary of £60 per day. 

You will experience excellent development opportunities – from a comprehensive induction to the Bank, through to a range of talks and networking events. If your performance during the six weeks is considered to be outstanding, you will be given the opportunity to be assessed for a Penultimate Year Internship.

Apply now for the First Year Internship.

Penultimate Year Internship

This scheme is for first-degree students in their penultimate year. Our undergraduate internships pay £65 per day and run for eight weeks. You'll focus on an individual project, working closely with economists and analysts to collect and analyse data. Graduate offers are made to outstanding performers at the end of the internship.

You will experience excellent development opportunities – from a comprehensive induction to the Bank, through to a range of talks and networking events.

 Apply now for the Penultimate Year Internship. 

Postgraduate Internship

This scheme is for students who have completed their first degree and are about to embark on their Masters year of study. The internship will run for eight weeks and you will be paid £65 per day.

Please note that this internship is no longer affiliated to the Postgraduate Sponsorship process as we have brought the timeline forward to the end or the Penultimate Summer so that we are able to confirm funding for Masters fees before application deadlines.
